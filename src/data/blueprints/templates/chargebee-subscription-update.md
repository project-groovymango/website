## Overview

When a customer upgrades or downgrades their plan, this automation updates the existing Chargebee subscription to match. Works when your quote-to-cash process is fully standardised in HubSpot.

## What's included

- HubSpot deal stage change listener
- Chargebee subscription lookup and update
- Plan change validation logic
- Proration handling
- HubSpot timeline event logging

## How it works

1. A deal in HubSpot moves to the upgrade/downgrade stage
2. The automation finds the matching Chargebee subscription
3. It updates the plan, add-ons, and quantities
4. Proration is calculated automatically
5. A timeline event is logged in HubSpot for the record
