OpenClaw for Sales Teams

An AI assistant that handles scheduling, follow-ups, and CRM updates — so your sales team focuses on closing deals.

Sales reps spend more time on admin than selling. CRM updates, scheduling calls across time zones, writing follow-up emails, preparing for discovery calls — it all adds up. OpenClaw automates the admin so your team spends more time in front of prospects and less time behind a keyboard.

The problem

CRM hygiene is a nightmare

Reps forget to log calls, update deal stages, and add notes. OpenClaw monitors email and calendar events, automatically updating your CRM with meeting notes, next steps, and deal progress.

Follow-ups fall through the cracks

A prospect says 'circle back next quarter' and the task disappears. OpenClaw tracks every follow-up commitment and drafts the outreach when the time comes — no more lost deals.

Scheduling wastes selling time

Back-and-forth emails to find a meeting time kill momentum. OpenClaw proposes times, handles reschedules, and confirms meetings — across any time zone.

Discovery call prep takes too long

Researching prospects, reviewing past interactions, preparing talking points. OpenClaw does this automatically before every call and sends you a pre-meeting brief.

What OpenClaw automates for sales teams

Automated follow-ups

After every meeting, OpenClaw drafts a personalized follow-up email based on the conversation. You review and send with one click.

Meeting scheduling

Prospects email asking for a call? OpenClaw proposes available times, handles the back-and-forth, and adds the event to your calendar with prep notes.

Pre-call briefing

15 minutes before each sales call, you get a Telegram message with the prospect's background, company info, previous interactions, and suggested talking points.

Pipeline monitoring

OpenClaw watches your inbox for signals — prospect replies, objections, competitive mentions — and flags deals that need attention.

Weekly sales summary

Every Friday, your sales lead gets a summary of all meetings held, follow-ups sent, deals advanced, and pipeline changes for the week.
