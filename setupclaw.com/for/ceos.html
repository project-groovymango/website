What OpenClaw automates for ceos & founders

Inbox triage

Every 30 minutes, OpenClaw scans your inbox. Urgent emails get flagged instantly. Routine emails get draft replies. Low-priority emails get archived with a summary.

Morning briefing

Every day at 9 AM, you get a message on Telegram with your agenda, flagged emails, attendee backgrounds, and anything that needs attention before your first meeting.

Investor update drafts

End of month approaching? OpenClaw pulls metrics from your connected tools and drafts your investor update. You review, edit, and send — saving 2–3 hours per month.

Meeting prep

Before each meeting, OpenClaw sends you attendee LinkedIn summaries, relevant email threads, and context from previous interactions. You walk in prepared, every time.

Follow-up nudges

Said 'I'll get back to you' in an email? OpenClaw tracks it and nudges you (or drafts the follow-up) when the deadline approaches.
